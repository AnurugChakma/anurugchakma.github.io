Teaching

Since 2013, I have taught and tutored a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses in political science, international relations, peace and conflict studies, Bangladesh Studies, and qualitative and quantitative research methods at the Australian National University (ANU), the University of Dhaka (DU), and Dhaka City College (DCC). Beyond teaching, I have delivered invited lectures on topics such as research methods, human rights, grassroots peacebuilding, and terrorism at academic institutions, including ANU, and training centres in Bangladesh, including the Ansar-VDP Academy, FIVDB Training Centre, and BRAC Learning Centre. My teaching philosophy emphasises critical pedagogy, real-world application, simulation, and inclusive student engagement.
